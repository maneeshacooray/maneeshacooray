### About Me

I’m an **Embedded Systems and Linux Engineer** passionate about **kernel development**, **open-source**, and **hardware-software integration**.
My work spans from **bare-metal firmware** to **custom Linux distributions** and **Rust-based embedded tooling**.
I build reliable and efficient systems for IoT, networking, and industrial applications.

- Experienced with **ESP-IDF**, **Zephyr**, **Yocto**, **PlatformIO**, and **Zig**
- Proficient in **C, C++, Rust, Python**, and **Bash**
- Skilled in **OpenWRT**, **Buildroot**, **Kernel configuration**, and **Driver debugging**
- Knowledgeable in **MQTT**, **ChirpStack**, **LoRaWAN**, and **real-time data systems**
- Familiar with hardware ecosystems: **RAK Wireless**, **Espressif**, **Khadas**, **BeagleBone**, **Luckfox**, **Orange Pi**, **Banana Pi**
